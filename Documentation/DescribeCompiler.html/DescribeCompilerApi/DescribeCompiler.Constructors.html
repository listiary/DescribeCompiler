DescribeCompiler
#Constructors

Those are the constructors of the compiler.


DescribeCompiler(string templateName)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity, Action<string> logText)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity, Action<string> logText, Action<string> logError)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity, Action<string> logText, Action<string> logError, Action<string> logInfo)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity, Action<string> logText, Action<string> logError, Action<string> logInfo, Action<string> logParserInfo)
DescribeCompiler(string templateName, LogVerbosity verbosity, Action<string> logText, Action<string> logError, Action<string> logInfo, Action<string> logParserInfo, IUnfoldTranslator translator)

templateName - The name of the templates set to use
verbosity - The logging verbosity of the parser
logText - method to log text
logError - method to log errors
logInfo - method to log less important info
logParserInfo - method to log parser output
translator - The translator to be used
